Kabaddi

Kabaddi is a contact team sport where two teams take turns raiding the other team's half of the court. The goal is to tag as many opponents as possible without getting caught.

Volleyball

Volleyball is a team sport where two teams of six players try to score points by grounding the ball on the other team's side of the net. It's a popular game played around the world.

Hockey

Hockey encompasses both ice hockey and field hockey, two sports with distinct rules and playing fields; ice hockey uses a puck and skates while field hockey uses a ball and sticks, both aiming to score by hitting the ball/puck into the opposing goal.

Badminton

Badminton is a fast-paced racket sport played by two players or teams on a rectangular court. The goal is to hit a shuttlecock over the net into your opponent's side of the court.
